1. Who the Hell Am I?

I'm Karar — just another dude who talks to machines better than people. I build websites because breaking the internet is apparently frowned upon. Been writing code since forever (well, since 2022, but close enough). HTML, CSS, JS, React, php — yeah, I speak all that nerdy stuff. If you're here looking for a "professional" bio full of buzzwords... you're in the wrong corner of the internet, my friend.

2. What I Actually Do (Besides Crying at 3AM Debugging)

Build Stuff from Scratch Websites, portfolios, dashboards, your aunt’s bakery page — whatever. I make pixels behave. Make Ugly Things Pretty UI design that doesn’t make your eyes bleed. You're welcome. Speed Things Up I slap the lazy out of slow websites. And yes, Google loves that. Fix Things I Probably Didn’t Break Maintenance and updates. I’ll even pretend to care.

3. My So-Called Projects

I've made some pretty cool stuff. Some of it even works. Portfolios, e-commerce sites, landing pages, random experiments at 2AM that somehow ended up on the internet. Click around if you're bored — maybe you'll find something that doesn't suck.
